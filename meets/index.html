Future Meets

Building our local community together

Our Meetup Plans

We're excited to start organizing regular fur-meets for yall! Here's how we plan to make this happen and what you can expect.

Community Goal

We're aiming to reach 100 members before organizing our first official meetup. This ensures we have a strong foundation of local furs to make meets successful and fun!

Local Venues

We'll be looking at furry-friendly venues across the Black Country area - cafes, parks, community centers, and other welcoming spaces for our meets.

Regular Events

Once we reach our goal, we plan to organize monthly meetups with a casual vibe.

Safe & Welcoming

All meets will be organized with safety and inclusivity in mind, ensuring everyone feels welcome and comfortable attending.

Future Meet Plans

Casual Coffee Meets

Relaxed meetups at local cafes for general chat and getting to know each other

Monthly Meetups

We plan to have a monthly meetup at a local venue, where furs can come and chat, vibe, and chill.

Fursuit Walks

We plan to have a fursuit walk in a local park, where furs can show off their fursuits and meet other furs

Gaming Sessions

Gaming sessions in a discord server for a variety of games such as Minecraft, VRChat, and more!

Art & Creative Corner

We plan creative section at meets for furs to show off their art, fursuit stuff and creative projects
